    Frutiger Aero

    The aesthetic is quickly explained by CARI, or the Consumer Aesthetics Research Institute including elements such as:


  • "Skeumorphism in UI/UX design

  • Glossy Design

  • Frutiger/humanist type sans-serif typefaces

  • Tertiary color palettes

  • Glassy/transparent materials

  • Photographs of aurora borealis

  • Bokeh Photography

  • Macro photographs of grass"

    •

    Frutiger Aero invokes the feeling of harmony, using both clean, white, and overall minimalist technology and architecture amidst open grassy fields and oceans.
    It had its start around the early 2000's, especially with Apple's newly debuted iPhone. Later on, gaming consoles like Nintendo's Wii and Sony's PlayStation 2/3 and its handheld, the PSP, became large contributions to the aesthetic.
